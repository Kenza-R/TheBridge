How It Works

Ambient detection.
Physician-controlled response.

Bridge works silently in the background, scoring three layers of signal. When something looks wrong, it surfaces one calm question — and three options, equally weighted.

Step 01
📊

EHR Signal Detection

Bridge reads your Epic workflow metadata — not patient data. After-hours charting volume, documentation time per note, adverse outcome documentation. No PHI. Ever.

Step 02
⌚

Wearable Biometrics

Apple Watch, WHOOP, Garmin — opt-in only. HRV trends, sleep quality, resting heart rate. Raw values never leave your device. Only a normalized score reaches our servers.

Step 03
🏷

Self-Report Tags

One tap from home screen. "Rough shift." "Lost a patient." "Running on empty." Optional, always. When you add one, it sharpens the signal dramatically.

Step 04
📡

Threshold Trigger

When your composite score crosses your personal threshold, a soft in-app banner appears — on next app open, no sound, no red badge. "You've had a tough stretch."

Step 05
⚖️

Three Options, Equal Weight

Reset Now — 90-second breathing exercise. Talk to Someone — your Circle of Trust. Handle Admin — dismiss or schedule a check-in. No option looks more urgent than another.

Step 06
🔒

Physician Decides

Bridge never auto-escalates. Never auto-notifies. Never sends anything without your explicit approval. You are always the actor. Always.

Circle of Trust

Your circle.
Your rules.
Your employer
never sees it.

During onboarding you build a private list of 2–6 trusted colleagues. The hospital cannot see who is in your circle. The hospital cannot see your messages. The hospital sees one number: utilization rate, no names.

👁
Full-screen message preview

Before anything sends, you see exactly what your colleague will receive. One "Send" tap. No accidental dispatch.

📅
Social recovery scheduling

Request a walk, a coffee, 15 minutes to debrief. Pulls from your shared Epic availability. One tap to confirm.

🔔
Third-party concern flagging

Worried about a colleague? Flag it privately. They see "a colleague is thinking of you." They never see your name.

Mental Health Space

A breath in the
middle of the storm.

Every tool available in 90 seconds or less. Built around physician reality — not the assumption that clinicians have 45 minutes and a quiet room.

🌊

90-Second Micro-Reset

Box breathing, 4-7-8, extended exhale. Full-screen animated orb that expands and contracts with your breath. Apple Watch haptic sync. Available directly from the trigger screen.

90 seconds
💬

3-Minute Guided Debrief

Three structured prompts: what happened, what you felt, what you'd tell a colleague in that moment. Text or voice. Stored locally on device. Never transmitted. Never seen by anyone.

3 minutes
🎧

Meditation Library

10 audio tracks built specifically for healthcare workers. "After the code." "End of shift exhale." "Night shift grounding." All offline — no streaming delay in the ICU.

2–5 minutes

RISE Peer Support

Johns Hopkins model. Trained peer responders available within 30 minutes of an adverse event. Anonymous request — you don't know which responder comes. Fully confidential.

Same-shift response
📈

Private Trend View

POST Coaching

We built Bridge so your employer can never see your struggle — only that the program is working.
🔐
Employer firewall

Hospital admin sees one number: aggregate utilization rate. No names. No departments below 10 people. No who-contacted-whom. This is contractual and enforced at the database level.

📱
Biometrics stay on-device

Raw HRV numbers, sleep minutes, heart rate — never transmitted to our servers. Your Apple Watch data computes a score locally. We receive one integer. Nothing more.

💬
Messages auto-delete

Peer messages expire after 30 days. End-to-end encrypted — the encryption key lives in your device Keychain. Our servers hold ciphertext only. We cannot read your messages.
