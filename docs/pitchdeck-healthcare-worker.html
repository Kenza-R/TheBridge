04 / 12
The Gap

Every existing solution
has a fatal flaw

  • EAPs go unused. Utilization rates below 5% among physicians — stigma, friction, and fear of employer visibility prevent access.
  • Generic wellness apps are forgotten. Push notifications ignored, daily check-ins abandoned — no connection to clinical workflow or real events.
  • Peer programs lack infrastructure. POST and RISE models are proven but manual, unscalable, and dependent on volunteer coordinators.
  • Surveillance fear kills trust. Physicians will not use any tool they believe reports back to administration. One data breach ends the product.
  • No event trigger. Every tool waits for the physician to self-identify distress. Burnout doesn't announce itself — it accumulates invisibly in the EHR.
05 / 12
The Solution

EHR-native. Peer-powered.
Privacy-by-design.

01
Detect — EHR Workflow Signal Monitors in-basket volume, after-hours EHR time, documentation burden, and adverse event flags within Epic. Detects burnout risk without any self-report.
02
Route — Circle of Trust Physician pre-selects 3–5 trusted peers. All routing decisions require live physician approval. Zero employer visibility. No alerts sent without explicit consent.
03
Support — Specialty Playbooks Post-operative complication debriefs for surgeons. ICU patient loss protocols. OB adverse event support. ED high-volume periods. Every specialty has its own workflow.
