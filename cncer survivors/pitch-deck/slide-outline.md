# Slide outline — Bridge Survivorship (seed)

**Format:** Investor deck (YC application + seed VC first meetings)  
**Audience:** YC partners, seed funds — 3-minute skim, 10-minute read  
**Goal:** Clear problem/solution, proof plan, bottom-up market, distribution, ask  

## Key message

**Bridge Survivorship is the post-treatment operating layer for community oncology** — starting with HR+ breast survivorship where symptoms, fear of recurrence, and coordination gaps are acute and measurable.

### Supporting arguments

1. Large underserved cohort (18.6M US survivors; survivorship capacity lags demand).  
2. Wedge beats super-app (protocolized breast HR+ in practice workflow).  
3. Venture path = B2B2C infrastructure with outcomes ROI, not content DTC.
